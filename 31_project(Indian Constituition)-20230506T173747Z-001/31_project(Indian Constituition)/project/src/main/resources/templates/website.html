Prominent Constitution

The Constitution of India is the supreme law of India. The document lays down the framework that demarcates fundamental political code, structure, procedures, powers, and duties of government institutions and sets out fundamental rights, directive principles, and the duties of citizens. It was adopted by the Constituent Assembly of India on 26 November 1949 and became effective on 26 January 1950.
